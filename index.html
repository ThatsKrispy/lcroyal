Accounting & Financials

About L&C Royal

South Florida's premier property management company.

L&C Royal Management has been serving communities across South Florida for more than 15 years. We are a full-service management company headquartered in Miami, dedicated to making a meaningful difference in every community we serve.

Our carefully selected team brings unique expertise in HOA administration, financial management, maintenance coordination, and owner relations. No two properties are the same — so we tailor our approach to match the specific needs and goals of each community.

Our mission is straightforward: strengthen your property's value, reduce operating costs, and create a comfortable, well-managed living environment that residents are proud to call home.
