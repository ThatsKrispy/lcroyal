Who We Are

Florida's Premier Property Management Company.

Welcome to L&C Royal Management — a full-service HOA and condo association management company headquartered in Miami, FL. We have been in business for more than 15 years and serve properties of all sizes across South Florida.

Our exceptional team is dedicated to approaching every service with immaculate detail, integrity, and proficiency. Since no two properties are alike, we provide a variety of services that allow communities to find solutions that fit their specific demands.

L&C Royal is made up of carefully selected associates — passionate individuals who bring unique skill sets and a genuine commitment to creating outstanding living environments for the communities we serve.
